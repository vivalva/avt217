CULTURE

Argentina is built on a rich culture. Every corner of the country has something different to offer, whether music, dance, or traditions. Immerse your self in a blooming culture, watch a tango or listen to street performers. There is also a huge soccer culture! Argentina is very passionate about sports, attending a soccer game is a must do! The sounds and energy of the games will keep you energized and engaged. Locals are very kind and accepting of foreigners, so do not be afraid to learn and engage with the community.
