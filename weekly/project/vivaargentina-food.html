FOOD

The food in Argentina is truly something special! Every corner of Argentina, from Buenos Aires to Cordoba you will find a delicious variety of traditional classics and twists on known food. One food you may find while on your travels is empanadas, a classic food consisting of pastry stuffed with a mixture of meat and veggies. Every empanada recipe is different, so there is a lot of variety in the kinds you will find! Another staple of Argentinean food is asado, or barbeque. Argentina is very serious about its red meat, so expect to see alot of steak, sausages, and morsilla (blood sausage).
